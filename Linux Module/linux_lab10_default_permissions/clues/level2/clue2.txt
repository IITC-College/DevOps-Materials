================================================================================
    OCTAL TO SYMBOLIC CONVERSION
================================================================================

Remember the octal to symbolic mapping:
  4 = read (r)
  2 = write (w)
  1 = execute (x)
  7 = 4+2+1 = read+write+execute (rwx)
  6 = 4+2 = read+write (rw)
  5 = 4+1 = read+execute (rx)
  0 = no permissions (---)

Example: 644 = rw-r--r--
  - 6 (4+2) = rw- (owner)
  - 4 = r-- (group)
  - 4 = r-- (others)
