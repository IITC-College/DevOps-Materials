--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
WHAT YOU'RE DOING
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
A *service* is "something that should run." On modern Linux, services are
managed by systemd. systemd keeps a list of services and their desired
state (running, stopped, etc.). The *process* is what actually runs; the
*service* is the "contract" that systemd enforces.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
