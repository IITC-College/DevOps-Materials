WHAT YOU'RE DOING
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
While lab-demo is running, systemctl --user status lab-demo shows "Main PID".
That PID is the **process**. The **service** is the unit systemd manages; the
**process** is what's actually running. When you stop the service, the process
goes away; when you start it again, a new process (new PID) appears.
